Baked Potato Soup

Description

Ever wanted to curl up with a good book when it's raining outside? This loaded baked potato soup will give you the same comfy feeling! This dish is perfect for having lunch on the patio while the leaves change color. One spoonful and the whole family will be begging you to write it down.

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bacon
  • 2/3 cup butter or margarine
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 7 cups milk
  • 4 large baked potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occassionally, until evenly browned, about 8 - 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels; crumble and set aside.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter in a stockpot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Gradually add flour, whisking until well-combined. Slowly pour in milk, whisking constantly until smooth and thickened.
  3. Add potatoes and onions and bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in crumbled bacon, Cheddar cheese, sour cream, salt, and pepper. Continue cooking and stirring until cheese is melted.
